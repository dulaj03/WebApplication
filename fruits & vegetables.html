Fruits & Vegetables Guide

Welcome to the World of Vegetable & Fruits

Discover the best Vegetables & Fruits for your home and learn how to take care of them.

Tomatoes - තක්කාලි

Tomatoes are one of the most widely grown vegetables, producing juicy, red fruits that are used in salads, sauces, and cooking.

Carrots - කැරට්

Carrots are root vegetables that grow underground and are rich in vitamins. They come in orange, purple, yellow, and red varieties.

Lettuce - සලාද කොළ

Lettuce is a leafy green vegetable used in salads, sandwiches, and wraps. It grows quickly and thrives in cool weather.

Bell Peppers - බෙල් පෙපර්

Bell peppers are colorful, crunchy vegetables that come in green, red, yellow, and orange varieties.

Apples - ඇපල්

Apples are one of the most popular fruits, coming in sweet and tart varieties. They grow on trees and require proper care for a good harvest.

Bananas - කෙසෙල්

Bananas are tropical fruits that grow in clusters on large plants. They are rich in potassium and commonly eaten fresh or used in baking.

Strawberries - ස්ට්‍රෝබෙරි

Strawberries are small, red fruits with a sweet and slightly tangy flavor. They grow in small bushes and spread through runners.

Oranges - දොඩම්

Oranges are juicy citrus fruits packed with vitamin C. They grow on evergreen trees and thrive in warm climates.

Tomatoes

How to Grow: Plant in well-draining soil with full sun (6–8 hours daily). Water consistently, keeping the soil moist but not soggy. Support plants with stakes or cages as they grow. Fertilize with a balanced fertilizer throughout the season. Harvest when fully red and slightly firm.

Carrots

How to Grow: Sow seeds directly in loose, well-draining soil. Keep the soil moist but not waterlogged. Thin seedlings to allow space for roots to develop. Harvest when carrots reach a desirable size (usually 60–80 days).

Lettuce

How to Grow: Plant in well-drained soil with partial to full sun. Water regularly to keep the soil moist. Harvest outer leaves continuously to encourage regrowth. Grow in containers for easy harvesting.

Bell Peppers

How to Grow: Plant in warm, well-drained soil with full sun. Water deeply but allow soil to dry slightly between watering. Stake plants to support heavy fruit production. Harvest when peppers reach the desired color.

Apples

How to Grow: Plant in well-drained soil with full sun. Space trees properly to allow airflow. Water deeply, especially in dry conditions. Prune to encourage strong branches and better fruiting.

Bananas

How to Grow: Plant in nutrient-rich, well-draining soil with full sun. Water regularly to keep the soil moist. Fertilize frequently for fast growth. Harvest when bananas are green and let them ripen off the plant.

Strawberries

How to Grow: Plant in well-drained soil with full sun. Keep the soil consistently moist. Mulch around plants to retain moisture and prevent weeds. Pick berries when fully red and ripe.

Oranges

How to Grow: Plant in well-draining soil with full sun. Water regularly, but allow the soil to dry between watering. Fertilize with citrus-specific fertilizer. Harvest when the skin turns bright orange.
